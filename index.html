Our Story ✦

Where Every Bite Feels Like Coming Home

Flavoured Bites was born out of one simple belief — street food should feel like a warm hug from your maa. We took the bold, tangy, spicy magic of Mumbai's chaat culture and wrapped it in love, freshness, and zero compromise.

Every masala is ground fresh. Every puri is fried to order. Every chutney is a secret recipe passed down generations. This isn't just food — it's a feeling.

🧼Hygiene-first kitchen — certified clean prep always
🌿Farm-fresh ingredients sourced daily
🚫🧪Zero artificial flavours — pure, real taste
🔥Customise your spice level — mild to deadly!
